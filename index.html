FONDERIA HD3

Foundry HD3 is a reality of reference in ALBANIA, in the city of Elbasan, for what concerns the processing of metals. The company offers foundry activities for cast iron and aluminum, following customer projects in a completely customized way. Thanks to the competent and well-trained staff of the sector, you will be able to take advantage of technical advice and a feasibility study for the realization of your metal product.

Our evaluation includes:
1 - assessment of the possibilities of supplying the requested quantities
2 - examination of the technical possibility of making the piece
3 - determination of the necessary equipment
4 - study of the casting and feeding system
5 - execution of sampling before batch production.

The Fonderia HD3 offers customers a complete service. We have the latest generation equipment and high professional skills, which allow us to build high-precision cast iron metal products. We are able to take care of every phase of the cast iron foundry, from the melting of the metal itself to the casting in the appropriate molds. In addition, our technical staff offers the aesthetic finish on die castings. At the FONDERIA HD3 we carry out a preventive feasibility study for the production of metal products in cast iron ,bronze, iron or aluminum.

Products

The company specializes in the production of lamellar and spheroidal cast iron castings used mainly for the production of the following products:
pumps;
1 - reducers;
2 - woodworking machines;
3 - glass processing machines;
4 - marble processing machines;
5 - paper processing machines;
6 - construction and agricultural machinery;
7 - earth moving machines;
8 - machines for the agri-food sector.

Mold Design

The life of a product depends a lot on the design of its mold, therefore the study and simulations are carried out to thoroughly analyze all possible problems, solving the problems at the root.

Fusion

Our foundries have decades of experience behind them and are specialized in all kinds of casting with any metal and production technology: from sand to die casting, from shell to lost wax.
